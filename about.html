Our Story

Why We Built PolishPoint

We kept seeing the same pattern: cleaning company owners paying $200-500 a month for software they didn't own, couldn't customize, and would lose everything if they ever stopped paying. That's not a tool — that's a trap.

We're developers who believe in a different model. Build it once, hand over the keys, charge fairly for infrastructure. No recurring revenue games, no feature-gating, no lock-in. The cleaning industry runs on tight margins and hard work — your software costs shouldn't eat into that.

PolishPoint is a product of Saasassins — a team that specializes in building modular, white-label business platforms at a fraction of what traditional agencies charge. We've stripped out the overhead and built a repeatable system that delivers enterprise-grade tools at small-business prices.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Ownership First

You pay for the build, you own the result. Your code, your database, your domain — no strings attached. Take it anywhere, modify it however you want.

Transparent Pricing

Every feature has a clear, one-time price. Infrastructure costs are at-cost with zero markup. We don't play pricing games because we don't need to.

Purpose-Built
